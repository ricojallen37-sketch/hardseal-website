Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 15, 2026
The short version:

Hardseal runs offline. Your compliance evidence never leaves your systems. We collect virtually nothing because the product doesn't phone home.

1. Who We Are

Hardseal is a compliance evidence engine built by A.I. Shovels LLC ("we", "us", "our"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect (very little), how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

2. The Product: Zero Data Collection

Hardseal is designed to run entirely offline in air-gapped and disconnected environments. During normal product operation:

  • No evidence data is transmitted to our servers or any third party
  • No telemetry is collected or sent
  • No usage analytics are gathered from the product
  • No system information leaves your environment
  • All cryptographic signing keys (Ed25519) are generated and stored locally on your systems

All evidence collection, processing, signing, and OSCAL export happens on your hardware, under your control. We have no access to your compliance data, system configurations, or evidence packages.

4. Email Communications

If you contact us at rico@hardseal.ai or through any business channel, we store your name, email address, and message content for the purpose of responding to your inquiry and maintaining a business relationship. We do not sell, rent, or share your contact information with third parties.

5. What We Do NOT Collect

  • System evidence or compliance data from your enclaves
  • Cryptographic keys or signatures generated by Hardseal
  • OSCAL output files or assessment artifacts
  • System configurations, network topology, or infrastructure details
  • Employee or personnel information from your organization
  • Behavioral analytics or usage patterns from the product

6. Voluntary Data Sharing

In limited circumstances, you may choose to share artifacts with us, such as sending OSCAL output files for troubleshooting or providing feedback that includes system details. Any such sharing is entirely voluntary and initiated by you. We will use voluntarily shared data only for the stated purpose and delete it upon request.

7. Data Security

For the minimal data we do collect (website logs, email correspondence), we employ reasonable security measures including encrypted email (DKIM-authenticated via Google Workspace), HTTPS for all web traffic, and access controls limiting data to authorized personnel only.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of any personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Objection: Object to processing of your data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at rico@hardseal.ai. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Third-Party Services

Our website is hosted on GitHub Pages. Our email is provided through Google Workspace. These services have their own privacy policies. We do not use any other third-party services that process user data.

10. Children's Privacy

Hardseal is a business-to-business compliance tool. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via email to known contacts and posted on this page with an updated effective date.
