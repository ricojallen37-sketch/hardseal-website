1. Service Description

Hardseal is an offline-first compliance evidence engine designed to assist Registered Practitioner Organizations (RPOs) and other authorized users in CMMC assessment support. The software collects system evidence, applies Ed25519 cryptographic signatures, and exports evidence packages in OSCAL v1.1.2 format to support the assessment process.

IMPORTANT:

Hardseal is assessment support software only. It is not a C3PAO (Cybersecurity Maturity Model Certification Assessor), does not perform assessments, does not provide legal, regulatory, or audit opinions, and does not guarantee CMMC certification or compliance outcomes.
