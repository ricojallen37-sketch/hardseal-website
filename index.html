The founder

Engineered by one founder.
AI-accelerated. Human-verified.

Hardseal exists because DIB contractors in air-gapped environments shouldn't need a six-figure SaaS subscription to prove compliance. The platform is built with AI-accelerated engineering under founder architectural review — every module is human-verified, security-audited, and tested against the NIST 800-171 Rev 2 control set before it ships. The thesis is the discipline: if one founder with the right architecture can deliver assessor-literate evidence this fast, that compresses the timeline and the cost for every contractor downstream.

Hardseal is assessment support software. It is not a C3PAO, does not certify organizations, and does not replace professional assessment judgment.
